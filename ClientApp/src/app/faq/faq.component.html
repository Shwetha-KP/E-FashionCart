Frequently Asked Questions

How does online tailoring with E-FashionCart work?
When you place an order, we'll schedule a pickup for your fabric & sample garment from your home. Next, a dedicated designer will help you style your fabric which you were selected the patterns from our source. Once the design is confirmed, we'll stitch the garment & ship it to your home in 5-15 working days. Alterations if any, are taken care of free of charge.
What kind of clothes do you stitch?
We stitch all types of women's, men's and kid's clothing, both ethnic & western. The categories we service include saree blouses, salwar kameez, lehengas, dresses, gowns, trousers and shirts, among other things. We also provide finishing services like saree fall addition, pico(zigzag), hemming & saree kuchu (tassel addition).
Do your prices include lining?
Yes, we don’t charge separately for lining. If a dress needs lining material, we will use it at no extra cost to you. We could make it without lining but it wouldn't be wearable right? Then why have a price for that? :)
Do you charge extra for pickup and delivery?
No, we do not. You just pay for the stitching & anything else you want us to buy on your behalf and that’s it.
Is it absolutly must for a tailor to take my measurements?
Although the self measurement can be done, we believe that the measurement is a critical aspect in getting the best fit so we have made it compulsory to get measured by one of the concierge stylists.
Do I need to give my measurements every time I order?
Yes. You should.
What if my tailored clothes don't fit as expected?
We’ll alter them at no extra charge based on the feedback you provide. And we’ll learn from the process by recording your final measurements for future use.
My mother wants some clothes stitched, but she is not tech-savvy. Can I place an order on her behalf?
Sure, go right ahead!
Can I have my suit tailored with my own designs: working cuffs, boot-cut trousers, a single vent, 3 buttons etc...?
Ofcourse. You can add your own designs.
